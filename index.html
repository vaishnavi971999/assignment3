Safety Measures Before You Go for Your Ice Climbing Experience

  • Belaying – Climbing without belaying is never advised. Belaying is the technique that keeps you from falling very far in case of a slip. Get in sync with your partner and clip the ropes properly with ice screws.
  • Proper Gears – Carry extra gloves, wear layers and make sure your equipment is well working. There is no predicting nature, so always wear a helmet.
  • Start early – You never know how long the climb may take. Always start early so it doesn’t get too dark while you’re still climbing.
  • Be prepared – While ice climbing, always be prepared for natural disasters such as avalanches, falling rocks and falling ice. Be cautious and observant, and also carry a first-aid kit just in case.
